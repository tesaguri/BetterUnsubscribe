BetterUnsubscribe Options

Email Unsubscribe Behavior

When disabled (default), a compose window will open for you to review before sending.
When enabled, the unsubscribe email will be sent automatically without review.

Require Confirmation

Show a confirmation dialog before unsubscribing when the target address matches this pattern.

Leave empty to disable confirmation prompts.
The pattern is case-insensitive and matches against the unsubscribe target "Name <sender@domain.com>".
